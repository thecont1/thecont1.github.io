<p class="dropcap">This is a fictional narrative exploring 21st century India through the lens of wedding culture. The project examines the contradictions and complexities of modern Indian society as reflected in the wedding industry, capturing the essence of a nation in transition.</p>

The work delves into the social, cultural, and economic dynamics that shape contemporary Indian weddings, offering a critical yet compassionate look at tradition versus modernity.

<p class="dropcap">Imagine a narrative that unfolds through a series of interconnected moments that reveal the tensions between tradition and modernity, wealth and poverty, and individual desires versus societal expectations.</p>
